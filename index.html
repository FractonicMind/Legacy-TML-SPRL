The Three States of Moral Logic

TML revolutionizes AI decision-making by introducing a third ethical state beyond binary allow/deny decisions.

+1

Permit

Clear ethical approval for action. The AI system has determined the decision is ethically sound and can proceed.

0

Sacred Pause

Moral complexity requires human oversight. The AI recognizes ethical nuance and pauses for human wisdom.

-1

Prohibit

Clear ethical rejection of action. The AI system has determined the decision would cause harm and blocks it.

Legal-Technical Framework

What TML Provides

  • Technical Specifications: Architectural patterns for ethical AI implementation
  • Legal Framework: Compliance requirements and accountability structures
  • Governance Standards: Oversight mechanisms and community governance
  • Protection Systems: Whistleblower and victim protection mechanisms
  • Audit Requirements: Complete transparency and documentation standards

Implementation Responsibility

Organizations implementing TML bear full responsibility for:

  • Technical implementation meeting TML specifications
  • Legal compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Operational safety and ethical use of AI systems
  • Staff training and competency verification
  • Harm prevention and victim compensation

Prohibited Applications

TML implementation is absolutely forbidden for:

  • Autonomous weapons systems or military targeting
  • Mass surveillance without judicial oversight
  • Social credit scoring or population control
  • Deceptive manipulation or misinformation campaigns
  • Financial fraud or market manipulation
