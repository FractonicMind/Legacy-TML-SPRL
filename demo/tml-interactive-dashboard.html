A Framework for Measured Accountability

Ternary Moral Logic (TML) quantifies risk via the Stakeholder Proportional Risk Level (SPRL), which triggers one of three states. This demo illustrates behavior; refer to the specs for normative requirements.

+1

Proceed

SPRL below threshold. Action proceeds with standard operational logging.

0

Sacred Pause

SPRL meets/exceeds the threshold; full Moral Trace Log is required.

-1

Prohibit

Universally unacceptable outcomes are blocked regardless of SPRL.
