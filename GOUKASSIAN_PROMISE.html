The Three Pillars of Purposeful Action

This framework provides a structure for ensuring any initiative is guided by purpose, executed with a unique identity, and empowered with the full authority to succeed. Explore the three pillars: The Lantern, The Signature, and The License.

Deconstructing the Pillars

Each pillar is built upon a foundation of key components. Understanding these is crucial to applying the framework effectively.

Lantern Components

  • Vision:The long-term, aspirational goal.
  • Mission:The core purpose and what you do.
  • Values:The non-negotiable guiding principles.

Signature Components

  • Style:The aesthetic and functional approach.
  • Voice:The tone and method of communication.
  • Process:The proprietary methods and workflows.

License Components

  • Authority:The formal permission to act.
  • Resources:The necessary assets (people, time, money).
  • Trust:The buy-in and confidence from stakeholders.
